# Privacy Policy

Tabatus does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Permissions

| Permission | Why |
|---|---|
| `tabs` | Query open tabs to populate the search list |
| `storage` | Save your preferences (fuzzy matching on/off) locally on your device |

No data ever leaves your browser. The extension makes no network requests.
